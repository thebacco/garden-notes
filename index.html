Quick Summary

Base Compost and worm castings

Use before planting or as a light side-dress to support soil structure.

Growth Fish emulsion or blood meal

Nitrogen-forward feeds for leafy growth. Go easy once fruiting starts.

Fruit Tomato/vegetable fertilizer

Balanced or bloom-leaning blends for tomatoes, peppers, and cucumbers.

Stress Kelp meal or liquid kelp

Useful around transplanting, heat stress, and recovery weeks.
